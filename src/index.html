The Game

The concept for this game was to build a minesweeper varient where you dig through the level, trying to avoid the mines to reach the next level.

Instructions

    Starting at the starting tile dig your way to the ending tile without discovering any bombs to reach the next level.
  • Select a to explore.
  • A space with Numbers gives you a hint to how many bombs are surrounding it.
  • You begin with 5 lives and lose a life if you find a bomb .
  • Right click to toggle a flag tiles on what you think may be bombs.
